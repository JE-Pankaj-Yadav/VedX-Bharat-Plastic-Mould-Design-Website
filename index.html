About VedX Bharat

Established in 2025 by experienced mould designers Prabal Dwivedi & Yashveer Singh

Where India shapes the future of the world with its knowledge

VedX Bharat is our trusted partner in custom plastic mould design. We provide end-to-end mould design solutions for plastic product manufacturers, ensuring durability, accuracy, and superior production performance.

We specialize in designing precision plastic moulds for industries including packaging, consumer electronics, automotive, medical and home appliances.

Our Services

Comprehensive mould design solutions for diverse industrial applications

Injection Mould Design

Custom mould designs for plastic components used in industrial, medical, and consumer applications.

CAD Modeling & Drafting

3D modelling, draft analysis, undercut resolution, parting line definition, and full 2D detailing.

Mould Flow Analysis

Advanced simulation services for gate optimization, fill pattern validation, and cycle time prediction.

Prototype & Tool Validation

Functional prototyping, pilot mould testing, and design adjustments for tool performance.

Mould Modification & Repair

Redesign or modify existing tools for new applications or improved functionality.

Blow Mould & Thermoforming

Specialized design services for blow moulding and thermoforming applications.
