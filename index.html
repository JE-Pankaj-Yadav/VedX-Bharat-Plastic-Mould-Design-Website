About VedX Bharat

Established on 1 January 2023 by experienced designers Yashveer Singh & Prabal Dwivedi

Where India shapes the future of the world with its knowledge

VedX Bharat is your trusted partner in custom plastic mould design. We provide end-to-end mould design solutions for plastic product manufacturers, ensuring durability, accuracy, and superior production performance.

We specialize in designing precision plastic moulds for industries including packaging, consumer electronics, automotive, medical and home appliances. Our experienced team combines technical skill with practical industry knowledge to turn concepts into production-ready moulds.

Our Services

Comprehensive design solutions for diverse industrial applications

Injection Mould Design

From 3D modeling to draft analysis, undercut resolution, parting line definition, and full 2D detailing — we deliver designs ready for manufacturing.

Sheet Metal Tool Design

Die design for stamping, bending and forming sheet metal components including progressive and compound dies.

CAD Modeling & Drafting

From 3D modeling to draft analysis and full 2D detailing, we transform concepts into precise, production-ready designs.

Mould Flow Analysis

Advanced simulation services for gate optimization, fill pattern validation, and cycle time prediction.

Prototype & Tool Validation

Functional prototyping, pilot mould testing, and design adjustments for tool performance.

Jigs & Fixtures

We design high-precision jigs and fixtures that simplify manufacturing, enhance productivity, and guarantee consistent quality.

Mould Modification & Repair

Redesign or modify existing tools for new applications or improved functionality.

Need custom tooling? We deliver design-ready files that reduce production hassle and maximize ROI.
